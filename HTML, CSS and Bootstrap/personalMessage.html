A Special Message From the Co-Creator of This Website

Dear Papa,

It's been a very interesting ride that you've had through your life so far. Simply put, I want you to know that many people, especially your family are thankful for what you have done for us. I know you feel like we are stuck in a hole right now and you are trying to dig us out, but know that we are very lucky to be where we are and know that you've been through hell and back so this is nothing for you! Thank you, truly, for everything that you've done to help those close to you, know that when I grow up, I will follow your footsteps in giving the best for those close to me. I am prouder than any son on Earth to have you as my dad.

Love,
Darryl

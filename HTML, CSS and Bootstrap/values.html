Introduction

Jonathan is a role model for all dads around the world for a reason. He keeps the right things close to heart! While he may not be the most social person you'll meet, when you get to know him, there are a multitude of traits which make him #1.

Family First

Jonathan goes through life with the philisophy that family is God. He believes that when you marry and have kids, it is a committment which cannot be broken! Having been married faithfully for 25 years he has shown time and time again that he can and will fight for the family and for what's right!

Work Until Finished

Jonathan also strongly believes in having a sound and efficient work ethic. Never leaving work undone, Jonathan has a work ethic which is rare to find in the world. His work ethic along with his intelligence has allowed him to consistently rise up in the corporate and personal world. Swiftly, Jonathan rose the ranks in the corporate office while still maintaining a sense of entrepenurship driving him to open Eye Level.

Get Ready For the Future

There is no better person than Jonathan to look to the future. Constatly making sure that the family will survive and thrive in the future and beyond, he is almost always looking for new opportunities albeit sometimes too much.

Conclusion

All these traits and more just make up a fraction of what makes Jonathan a dad which everyone looks up to and aspires to be.

Roots

Jonathan, in spite of all the accomplishments that he has acheived was born into a relatively impoverished family. Born in Pasuruan, Indonesia his family lived a rough life not being able to have the luxuries like in the first world countries. Growing up however, Jonathan wasn't satisfied with the current position and wanted more!

Rising Up

Against all odds, Jonathan took up the challenge of bettering the lives of those who he loves and set his eyes on the Western World. Continuously pushing himeself Jonathan travelled and stayed in Australia for a couple years before finally settling in the United States.

Through Hell and Back

After settling into life in the States, Jonathan and his wife decided to open up a business in order to bring in income. His wife decided to start up an assisted living. After suffering losses for seven years, life finally looked brighter. Throughout the seven years however Jonathan and his family tried their best to stay positive and made it through.

Current Position

Recently Jonathan decided to open a second business, this time being a learning center called Eye Level, his wife still runs the assited living and they are now a happy family with three kids. Jonathan has been a role model for many dads around the world, tanking through the hard times and keeping the family together!
